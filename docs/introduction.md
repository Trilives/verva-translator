# Introducing Verva Translate

> Carry the meaning. Keep the voice.
> 译出含义，也保留语气。

## Why it exists

Verva began with deep dissatisfaction with machine translation that looks correct but fails in use. The vocabulary may survive while context, warmth, precision, and the speaker's voice disappear.

There is also a problem of control. Translators with useful AI features often require an expensive monthly subscription to cover model calls. Even after paying, users may be unable to choose the endpoint, bring their own key, select the model, or pay only for actual usage.

Verva is based on three principles:

1. Meaning is necessary, but tone and intent are also part of the message.
2. Users should explicitly control how a translation sounds.
3. Users should control the model, endpoint, and usage cost.

The application is intentionally focused: a clean Windows workspace, practical styles, direct compatible API access, and careful local security. It does not pretend to translate without AI and does not hide model costs inside another mandatory subscription.

## 中文介绍

Verva 的起点，是我对机器翻译的强烈不满。很多译文看起来没有明显错误，实际使用时却丢失了语境、温度、准确程度和说话者本人的语气。

另一个问题是选择权。真正具备实用 AI 能力的翻译工具，往往通过昂贵月费承担模型调用成本。即使已经付费，用户仍可能无法自带密钥、选择端点和模型，也不能只为实际用量付费。

Verva 建立在三个原则之上：

1. 含义很重要，但语气和意图同样属于信息的一部分。
2. 译文如何表达，应当由用户明确控制。
3. 模型、接口和调用成本，应当由用户自行选择。

这是一款刻意保持专注的 Windows 工具：提供清晰的工作区、实用风格、直接兼容接口和谨慎的本地安全设计。它不会假装在没有 AI 的情况下完成翻译，也不会把模型费用隐藏在另一项强制订阅中。
